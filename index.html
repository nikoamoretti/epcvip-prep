EPCVIP — The Business

Fintech lead gen, $200M+ revenue, proprietary platform

What They Do

Technology company in financial services lead generation. Two-sided marketplace: publishers drive consumer traffic, lenders buy qualified leads. EPCVIP sits in the middle, makes money on the spread. Also generates first-party traffic (own PPC, SEO, email) — highest margin, full control.

The Marketplace Flow

CONSUMER searches for a loan | +-----------+-----------+ | | EPCVIP 1st-party External publishers Own PPC, SEO, email NerdWallet, Credible Highest margin Media buyers, affiliates | | +--------+--------+ | EPCVIP PING-POST ENGINE Real-time auction <1.5s Proprietary platform | +------------+------------+ | | | Online Debt & Auto & Lenders Credit Other Personal Debt consol Auto fin Install. Credit mon Auto ins Business EWA (new) Merchant 100+ downstream lenders via Credible (Goldman Sachs, SoFi, LendingClub, Upstart...)

Ping-Post Mechanics

Only explain if he tests you.

  1. Consumer fills form on landing page (EPCVIP-owned or affiliate)
  2. Consent captured via TrustedForm
  3. PII stripped → ping created with attributes only (state, ZIP, credit range, loan amount, income, employment)
  4. Ping broadcast to all matching buyers simultaneously
  5. Buyers bid within milliseconds
  6. Platform decides exclusive vs shared based on total revenue max
  7. Winner(s) receive the post: full PII + consent cert
  8. If rejected → waterfall to next bidder (recovery revenue)

Key metrics: fill rate, acceptance rate, return rate, EPC

The Flywheel

Higher EPCs attract better publishers → better publishers send higher-quality traffic → higher-quality traffic gets higher buyer bids → higher buyer bids fund higher EPCs → cycle repeats. First-party pages add a margin accelerator (no publisher payout = full spread).
